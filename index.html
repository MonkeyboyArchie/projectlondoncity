We are Project London City

What does Project London City Offer?

1) Customized Assets: From unique vehicles and weapons to tailor-made clothing designed to enhance your roleplaying experiance.

2) Active Development & Updates: Project London City is constantly evolving. We are committed to providing you with fresh content, exciting features, and regular updates.

3) Responsive Staff Team: Our Staff Team is always here to assist you. Whether you need help resolving an issue, want to report a bug, or have a suggestion to improve the server, we value your feedback and are here to make your experience exceptional.

4) Safe and Secure Environment: We prioritize a safe gaming environment for everyone. Our server is equipped with advanced anti-cheat systems and vigilant moderators to ensure fair play and an enjoyable experience for all 

5) Diverse RP Scenarios: Explore a wide range of roleplaying scenarios, from theft to fires.
Project London City has its own unique 999 system to submit calls to on duty officers.

Our Assets

At Project London City we have hundreds of custom emergency service vehicles which are easily assessable by every member of the community.
Members of the community can access our wide range of vehicles by typing in chat /vs which opens the vehicles spawner menu.

Clothing

At Project London City we have hundreds of custom clothing for members to wear and roleplay in.
The clothing which we have is the same as what the real service uses.

Scripts

At Project London City we have hundreds of custom scripts designed to enhance your roleplay.
Some of our custom scripts give members support on what to say or do in sertain situations.
