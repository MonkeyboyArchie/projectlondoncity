Project London City Rules

Section One: Discord Rules

1.1 - Discord TOS 
• All content must adhere to the Discord Terms of Service. 
1.2 - General Rules 
• Use the channels as described in both the name of the channel and the channel topic found at the top of the channel. 
• Do not add members from our Discord server with the sole intention of advertising other`communities or servers. 
• Do not post IP trackers or other malicious gathering tools. 
• Do not microphone spam in a voice channel, this includes but is not limited to playing music down your microphone, screaming into your microphone, using your microphone for anything other than a conversation with the other members in the voice channel. 
• Do not tag or message staff members if not mutually agreed upon. Create a support ticket if you need a staff member's assistance. 

Section Two: FiveM Game Rules 

2.1 - RDM 
• Do not kill or attempt to damage someone without a valid RP reason. • Friendly fire is prohibited under any circumstances. 
2.2 - FailRP 
• Doing something in a roleplay situation that anyone in real life wouldn't realistically do • Talking out of character over IC-ideas, such as voice chat and vise versa.
2.3 - Minge (NITRP) 
• Do not mess around on the server, use common sense. 
2.4 - Metagame 
• Achieving knowledge out of an RP situation and bringing it into an RP situation. 
2.5 - Moderation Compliance 
• A staff member's ruling is final and failure to follow their rulings will result in punishment. 
2.6 - Leaving To Avoid Punishment 
• Leaving/Disconnecting from the server to avoid a roleplay situation or to avoid punishment is not allowed. 
2.7 - Microphone 
• You must have a working microphone to play on our server. 
2.8 - Age 
• The minimum age to be a member of the community is 13 years old 

Section Three: OUT OF CHARACTER RULES (OOC) 

3.1 - Disrespect 
• Do not be insulting towards other members of the community. 
• Do not impersonate anybody else on the server. 
3.2 - Discrimination 
• Any form of Discrimination is not permitted on the server. 
3.3 - OOC/IC (Out of Character / In Character) Spam • Spamming chat in-game including both in character and out of character 
• Spamming the microphone in-game including music, continuous transmission, and general mic spamming. 
3.4 - Violence and Harassment 
• Do not promote violence or harassment. 
• You may promote terrorism in an RP situation as any form of a terrorist organization. 
• You can not continuously annoy a member of the community, no matter other scenarios.
3.5 - Advertising 
• Do not promote any community or website, this will result in a permanent ban. 
3.6 - Hacking 
• Do not use any third-party modifications, this will result in a permanent ban. 
3.7 - Lying to Staff 
• Intentionally giving staff false information.
